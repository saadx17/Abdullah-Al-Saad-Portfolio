LIFE IN THE HAOR
IS A PARADOX.

The sun bears down with a merciless glare, the air thick with thirst. There are no roads—only water, endless and shifting.

Boats are not a choice, but a necessity. Life here is raw, stripped to its bones.
Yet within the hardship, there's something else—something haunting.
The haor wears its beauty like a storm: fierce, unpredictable, and breathtaking. It doesn't ask to be admired; it dares you to understand it.

SOMEWHERE IN BETWEEN

Somewhere in Between is my debut novel, currently a work in progress. It begins with the sincere journal entries of a young boy who shares his feelings and experiences of how others treat him.

The story follows his journey as bullying incidents shift his reputation at school, plunging him into chaos and self-doubt.
In the midst of the chaos, he finds an unexpected best friend who becomes a vital source of support.
With surprising twists along the way, the story follows his ongoing journey of growth, resilience, and the search for identity in the uncertain spaces between childhood and self-discovery.
